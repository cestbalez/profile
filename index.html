My favorite albums

Madvillain - Madvillainy (2004)

A collaboration project with MF Doom and the infamous producer Madlib from Stones Throw Records. It is an experimental Hip Hop album that contains elements from jazz, raggae, and much more, but with a definite stoner mood attached to it.

DJ Shadow - Endtroducing (1996)

DJ Shadow's Endtroducing recvolutionized the electronic music scene in the mid 90's, and was the first album to be 100% sample_based. It is a melodious and balanced record, which paints an urban 90's landscape with it's tunes.

Cannibal Ox - The Cold Vein (2001)

Cannibal Ox is the definition of a supergroup, and is formed from underground producer EL-P, and the MC's Vordul Mega and Vast air. It has a dark theme throughout, heacy electronic beats and serious lyrics
